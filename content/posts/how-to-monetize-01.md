How to monetize a media business, then vs now.

Old way:
- Text ads.
- Banner ads.
- Take-over ads.
- Pop-up ads.

New Way:
- Sponsored content.
- Paid newsletters.
- Information products.
- Membership communities.

Advertising is only good if it creates value for your *audience* as well as your advertisers.
